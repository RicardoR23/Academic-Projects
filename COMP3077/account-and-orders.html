Account and Orders

Create an account

Open the Register page, enter a name, email address, and password with at least 8 characters, then submit the form.

Login

After registration, sign in from the Login page. Customers go to their profile; admins are redirected to the dashboard.

Checkout and order history

Checkout requires a signed-in account. When the order is placed, it is written to the orders and order_items tables. Users can later review the order on the Orders page.

Seeded admin account

The starter SQL file includes an admin user for testing: admin@creatorkart.local with password Admin123!. Change it after deployment.

Account guide complete.
