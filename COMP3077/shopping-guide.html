Shopping Guide

How to shop

  1. Browse the catalog or use the search box.
  2. Open a product page.
  3. Select the first option and second option.
  4. Choose quantity and click Add to cart.
  5. Review the cart and proceed to checkout.

Reviews and ratings

Logged-in users can rate products from 1 to 5 stars and leave a short text review on each product page.

Product comparison

The comparison page lets users compare several items by category, stock, and option labels.

Updating content without programming

To change a product image or video, replace the file inside assets/img/products or assets/media and, if needed, update the matching database field in the admin editor or directly in MySQL.
